planetsca.simplify_aoi
=====================

Functions to simplify a geometry for an area of interest (AOI). Searching for Planet imagery via their API using a geometry filter requires that the geometry is `not too complex <https://developers.planet.com/blog/2022/Dec/15/simplifying-your-complex-area-of-interest-a-planet-developers-deep-dive/>`_.
